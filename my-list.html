1.
Lebanese Cuisine

No. of tags: 1.083 million

Lebanese food shares elements with other Middle Eastern food, such as grains, dried fruits, legumes like chickpeas, and herbs and spices.

There is also an abundance of fresh seafood, courtesy of the country's long coast. Olive oil is used on most dishes, though lemon juice is also typical.

Best lebanese food to try :

Try a juicy and tender shawarma if you're in the mood for something very filling. Or, for something lighter, go for fattoush, a light but delicious pita salad.

2.
Indian Cuisine

No. of tags: 11.339 million

Over 11 million people are tagging Indian cuisine, and no one is shocked. A global favorite, Indian food is varied, flavorful and unique. Curries, tandoori meats and fluffy naan can be found all over the world, in part due to the large Indian diaspora.

Another thing that makes Indian food so popular is that it's vegetarian-friendly, with most dishes offering veggie options wherever you are.

Best Indian food to try :

Within India, each region has its own very marked cuisine. Mumbai cuisine is popular outside of the country and especially in Indian restaurants in the United States.

Try dishes like baida roti, a stuffed pastry that is usually eaten as a snack or appetizer.

2.
Italian Cuisine

No. of tags: 17.927 million

We always knew Italian food would be a contender for the top spot, but on Instagram, it missed first place by a narrow margin.

Italian cuisine has become ubiquitous around the world, with pasta and pizza getting adopted by almost every other country as common foods and ranking amongst the top three European dishes.

Best Italian food to try :

While in Italy, you'll find plenty of other delicious dishes, like polpette, Italiano pasta, pizza and risotto, a creamy rice dish.

Italy has also gifted the world one of the most simple yet delectable desserts ever invented: gelato.

2.
French Cuisine

No. of tags: 1.736 million

Typically considered one of the finest cuisines in the world, French food is a leader in world gastronomy

Traditional dishes tend to be buttery and oily, yet manage not to be heavy and to triumph in the difficult game of subtle tastes. Some must-try dishes include escargot, or snails, which are so much more delicious than you'd think, as well as frog legs and apple and blood sausage.

Best French food to try :

But where French cuisine really shines is in the desserts, which have changed the way the rest of the world eats sweets.

Head to a bakery and order pan au chocolat, macarons, religieuses or — if you want to bring it back to basics — a perfectly baked croissant.

2.
Thai Cuisine

No. of tags: 8.13 million

We could go on and on about the wonders of Thai cuisine, so we are not surprised to see it rank within the top five most popular cuisines.

Make sure you try the spicy shrimp soup tom yum goong, green and red curries, khao pad fried rice and the amazing dessert, mango sticky rice.

Best Thai food to try :

Pad Thai may have been invented in the 1930s, but it's still one of the best foods in the entire world.

Like tacos, it's a cliche for a reason, and it's one of the country's most absolutely delectable dishes.
